# Privacy Policy for Vocaberry

**Last Updated:** January 26, 2026

## Introduction

Welcome to Vocaberry ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application Vocaberry (the "App").

By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use the App.

## Information We Collect

### 1. Personal Information
We may collect the following types of personal information:

- **Account Information**: Username, email address, and profile information you provide when creating an account
- **User-Generated Content**: Photos you upload for word associations, custom vocabulary lists, and learning progress data
- **Audio Recordings**: Voice recordings for pronunciation practice and assessment (processed and not permanently stored)

### 2. Automatically Collected Information
When you use the App, we automatically collect:

- **Device Information**: Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **Usage Data**: Features used, time spent in the app, learning statistics, practice sessions
- **Performance Data**: App crashes, error logs, and diagnostic information

### 3. Permissions We Request

The App may request the following permissions:

- **Microphone**: To record your voice for pronunciation practice and assessment
- **Camera/Photo Library**: To allow you to upload images for word associations and personalized learning
- **Storage**: To save your learning progress and downloaded content locally on your device
- **Internet Access**: To sync your progress, download content, and access online features

## How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information for the following purposes:

### Learning and Practice
- Provide personalized vocabulary learning experiences
- Assess and improve your pronunciation through audio analysis
- Track your learning progress and statistics
- Generate practice sessions based on your performance

### App Improvement
- Analyze usage patterns to improve app features and user experience
- Debug and fix technical issues
- Develop new features and content

### Communication
- Send you important updates about the App
- Respond to your inquiries and support requests
- Notify you about your learning achievements and milestones

## Audio Recording and Processing

### Pronunciation Assessment
- Audio recordings are used solely for pronunciation evaluation
- Recordings are processed using Azure Speech Services or similar third-party APIs
- Audio files are temporarily stored during processing and deleted immediately after assessment
- We do not permanently store or share your voice recordings

## Data Storage and Security

### Local Storage
- Your learning progress, vocabulary lists, and preferences are stored locally on your device
- You can delete this data at any time by uninstalling the App or clearing app data

### Cloud Storage
- Some data may be synced to our secure servers to enable cross-device functionality
- We use industry-standard encryption to protect data in transit and at rest
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to prevent unauthorized access

### Data Retention
- We retain your personal information only as long as necessary to provide our services
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time
